Skilet lasagna

Description

This is a no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2(28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1(14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clover garlic. minced
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil(Optional)
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano(Optional)
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoons black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.

  2. Step 2

    3. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook mafalda noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.

  3. Step 3

    4. dd cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.

  4. Step 4

    5. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.

  5. Step 5

    6. Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes.
